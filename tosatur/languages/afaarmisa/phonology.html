Phonotactics

Syllable structure is (C)VC(C)

Onset

All consonants allowed, no stops after [f], [ʒ], and [x], no stops after nasals, fricatives after stops are allowed, only stops allowed after [ɾ]

Nucleus

All vowels and all diphthongs allowed

Coda

All consonants except [x], [ɬ]. Any vowel or diphthong allowed

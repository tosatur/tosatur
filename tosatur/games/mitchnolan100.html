mitch nolan simulator 100: the new world

mitch nolan simulator 100: the new world is a parkour racing game i made in 2021.

it was intended as a reboot of the mitch nolan series; (this is somewhat meaningless as every entry in the mitch nolan franchise was completely disconnected from the rest in both genre, gameplay, and "plot.") i had the idea for this game in the shower.

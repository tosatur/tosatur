penis.africa

penis.africa (formerly bruh.website) is a general purpose news/shopping/public safety website set in a dystopian sci-fi world. it was written entirely in php, with mysql as the backend.

it features a blog, "bounties", a merch store, and artworks. it also used to feature a user account system, where users could log in and collect "hallam coins (hcn)". users would be ranked by the amount of hallam coins on their account.

the store was shutdown in 2022 due to security concerns.

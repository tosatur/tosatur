photography

in january 2023 i went on a road trip with my family up to the northern territory of australia, and took these photos on the way. we stopped in warnambool, adelaide, coober pedy, alice springs, uluru, and king's canyon. we enjoyed it a lot, the beautiful, diverse landscapes are something everyone should see at least once. although i wouldn't recommend it to anyone who doesn't like hot weather; it approached 47 degrees celsius at points.
